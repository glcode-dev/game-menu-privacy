# Privacy Policy

**Game Menu** does not collect any user information, analytics data, or usage statistics.  
No personal information is transmitted to the developer or any third parties.  

All data created or managed within the app remains on your device.  
If you enable iCloud sync, your data is securely stored in your personal iCloud account, managed by Apple.  
The developer of Game Menu cannot access any of your iCloud data.
